Shivneri Fort

Shivneri Fort is the birthplace of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. It is located near Junnar in Maharashtra.

The fort is well protected with strong walls and multiple gates. It also has water storage systems and temples.

Shivneri holds great historical importance and represents the early life of Shivaji Maharaj.

The architecture of Shivneri Fort is a brilliant example of a "Giridurg" (hill fort), designed to be virtually impregnable. Built in the triangular shape typical of the Yadava period, the fort is guarded by seven massive gates—including the Ganesh, Hanuman, and Kulambat gates—which any invader would have had to breach sequentially while under fire from the ramparts above. Located near Junnar in the Pune district, the fort sits atop a steep hill with sheer rock faces on all sides, ensuring that it remained a safe haven for the royal family during turbulent times.
