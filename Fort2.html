Famous Forts

Raigad Fort

Capital of Shivaji Maharaj’s empire, Raigad Fort was the site of his coronation in 1674.

Sinhagad Fort

Famous for the battle of Tanaji Malusare and located near Pune.

Pratapgad Fort

Known for Shivaji Maharaj’s victory over Afzal Khan in 1659.

Shivneri Fort

Birthplace of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, located near Junnar.

Murud-Janjira

A powerful sea fort that was never conquered despite multiple attacks.
